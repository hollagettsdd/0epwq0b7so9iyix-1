<p><p>The metal forming machine tools market comprises three main types: bulk forming, sheet forming, and powder metal forming. Bulk forming involves shaping large volumes of metal, such as forging, where material is manipulated under pressure. Sheet forming focuses on the processing of thin metal sheets through techniques like bending and stamping. Powder metal forming utilizes metal powders that are compacted and heated to create parts, offering design flexibility and efficient production for complex shapes. Each type serves distinct industrial applications and requirements.</p></p>
