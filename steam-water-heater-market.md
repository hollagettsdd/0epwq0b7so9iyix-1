<p><ul><li>Instantaneous Steam Water Heater</li><li>Semi-instantaneous Steam Water Heater</li><li>Storage Steam Water Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam water heater market encompasses three main types: instantaneous steam water heaters, which provide hot water on demand without storage; semi-instantaneous steam water heaters that combine both on-demand heating and a small storage capacity for immediate access to hot water; and storage steam water heaters, which hold a larger volume of heated water for use over time. Each type caters to different needs in residential and commercial settings, emphasizing efficiency, convenience, and continuous hot water supply.</p></p>
