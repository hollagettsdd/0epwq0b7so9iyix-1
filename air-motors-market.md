<p><strong>The Air Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vane Air Motor</li><li>Piston Air Motor</li><li>Gear Air Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air motors market consists of three main types: vane air motors, piston air motors, and gear air motors. Vane air motors use rotating vanes to create torque, offering smooth operation and high efficiency, making them ideal for light-duty applications. Piston air motors utilize a series of pistons for power generation, suitable for heavy-duty tasks with high torque needs. Gear air motors rely on gear reduction for increased torque output, making them effective in applications requiring precise control and higher speeds.</p></p>
