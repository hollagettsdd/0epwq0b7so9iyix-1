<p><strong>The Insulin Infusion Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed-Loop</li><li>Open-Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The insulin infusion pumps market is categorized into closed-loop and open-loop systems. Closed-loop pumps automatically adjust insulin delivery based on continuous glucose monitoring, providing a more precise insulin dosage and reducing the burden of manual management. Open-loop pumps require users to manually calculate and administer insulin doses, offering more control but necessitating vigilant monitoring. Both types cater to varying patient needs, with closed-loop systems increasingly gaining popularity for their automation and ease of use in managing diabetes effectively.</p></p>
